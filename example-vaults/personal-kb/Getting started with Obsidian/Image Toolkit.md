This is a great plugin that allows for zooming, panning, and looking deeply into images. A must-have for my workflow.

**Settings → Community plugins → Turn off Restricted mode** (one-time step, lets you install third-party plugins)

Then:

1. Click **Browse** in the Community plugins section
2. Search for **Image Toolkit**
3. Click **Install** → then **Enable**

That's it. After enabling, clicking any image in your notes will open it in a zoomable overlay.
